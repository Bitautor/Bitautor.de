This Privacy Policy outlines how we gather, employ, store, and safeguard personal data furnished by users via the App. While we prioritize the protection of your personal information, it's crucial to understand that no system is entirely immune to security breaches, and there are inherent risks associated with data handling. II. COLLECTED INFORMATION

A. Users should be aware that the sharing of personal information, even within a secure environment, carries inherent privacy and security risks. Exercise caution when providing this information.

B. Location Data: The App may access users' location data to provide them with information related to cannabis consumption safety zones in their vicinity. This access is exclusively granted with users' explicit consent, and the feature can be enabled or disabled in the App's settings. While location data is crucial for the proper functioning of the App, users should recognize the potential risks of sharing their location information and exercise discretion in granting consent.

C. Usage Data: We collect information concerning how users interact with and utilize the App, including the information they request, their in-App interactions, and technical data, such as device IDs, IP addresses, and browser types. This data is essential for improving the App's functionality and user experience. Nevertheless, sharing such technical information, even for improvement purposes, carries inherent risks, and users should be mindful of this when using the App.

III. PURPOSE OF DATA COLLECTION

A. Service Provision: We employ users' personal data to deliver the App's services, including the display of information related to cannabis consumption safety zones. This includes enabling users to receive geographically-relevant information and resources related to the legal and responsible use of cannabis in Germany. Users should understand that the dissemination of such information involves certain legal, privacy, and security risks.

B. Communication: With users' explicit consent, we may use their contact information to provide them with updates about the App or other pertinent information. Users have the option to opt out of these notifications at any time. Users should recognize that even communication regarding updates carries certain risks, and they should evaluate the information shared with discretion.

IV. SHARING YOUR INFORMATION

A. Third-Party Sharing: We do not share users' personal data with third parties without their explicit consent, unless legally mandated. While we prioritize data confidentiality, users should understand that the activities and data practices of third-party service providers, if used, can pose potential privacy and security risks.

V. DATA SECURITY

We institute and maintain suitable technical and organizational measures to ensure the security of user data and to prevent unauthorized access, loss, or theft. These measures include encryption, secure storage, and regular security assessments. However, it's crucial to understand that no security measure can guarantee absolute protection, and there is an inherent risk of unauthorized data breaches. Users are encouraged to exercise caution when sharing personal information or engaging in discussions within the platform.

VI. DATA RETENTION

We retain users' personal data only for the duration necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected, unless a legal requirement necessitates a more extended retention period. We have procedures in place to delete or anonymize personal data when it is no longer needed for its intended purpose. Users should be aware that data retention, even for legitimate purposes, carries potential privacy implications, and they should consider these when using the App.

VII. GEOGRAPHIC LIMITATION

The use of the CannaRadius App is exclusively permitted within the geographic boundaries of Germany. Access to the App is restricted outside of Germany, and any attempts to access or use the App from outside Germany may result in blocked or limited functionality. Users outside of Germany should understand that using the App may entail certain legal and privacy risks.
